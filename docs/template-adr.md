# Title

## Status

What is the status, such as proposed, accepted, rejected, deprecated,
superseded, etc.?

## Context

- What is the issue that we're seeing that is motivating this decision or
  change?

## Challenge

What are the specific difficulties or trade-offs that need to be addressed in
order to implement this decision?

## Goals

- What are the specific objectives that this decision aims to achieve?
- How will we measure the success of this decision?

## Risks

- What are the potential negative outcomes or unintended consequences of this
  decision?
- How likely are these risks to occur, and what would be their impact?

## Decision

- What is the change that we're proposing and/or doing?

## Consequences

What becomes easier or more difficult to do because of this change?
